About Color Blindness

Red-Green Color Blindness (Deuteranopia/Protanopia): Difficulty distinguishing between reds and greens.

Blue-Yellow Color Blindness (Tritanopia): Difficulty distinguishing between blues and yellows.

Complete Color Blindness (Achromatopsia): Inability to see colors at all, seeing only in shades of gray.
